Oxidation Reduction Chatbot

AI-Powered Chemistry Learning Assistant

Overview

The Oxidation Reduction Chatbot is an intelligent conversational AI designed to help high school and college students understand oxidation-reduction (redox) reactions in chemistry. The chatbot provides interactive explanations, answers questions, and guides students through complex chemical concepts in a friendly, accessible manner.

Key Features

  • Step-by-step explanations of redox reactions
  • Practice problems with instant feedback
  • Natural language processing for better understanding

Technologies Used

HTML CSS Flexbox JS

Challenges & Solutions

One of the main challenges was creating a natural conversation flow while maintaining chemical accuracy. I solved this by implementing a knowledge base of verified chemistry concepts and combining it with intent recognition to understand student questions in various phrasings.

Future Improvements

Plans include expanding to other chemistry topics, adding voice interaction, integrating with classroom management systems, and implementing personalized learning paths based on student progress.
